How to Use IndicLinguo

Learning a language often becomes easier and more natural through conversation. IndicLinguo is designed to help you do just that! It allows you to practice Indian languages by engaging in realistic role-playing scenarios with an AI partner. Here's how to get started:

1. Select Language

Choose the Indian language you want to practice from the first dropdown menu (e.g., Hindi, Telugu, Tamil).

2. Select Scenario

Pick a conversational scenario from the second dropdown menu. This sets the context for your practice (e.g., ordering at a Restaurant, checking into a Hotel, or just General conversation).

3. Start Conversation

Click the Start Conversation button. The chat area will activate.

Tip: You can also use the Quick Demo button to see an example conversation in Hindi.

4. Interact with the AI

Type your messages in English in the input box at the bottom of the chat.

Press Enter or click the Send button (looks like a paper plane ).

5. Understand the Response

The AI's response will appear in the chat. You'll see several parts:

  • AI Response (English): The AI's direct reply in English.
  • Translation: The AI's response translated into your chosen Indian language script.
  • Transliteration: The translation written using Roman (English) letters, helping you pronounce it.
  • Audio: Click the play button () to hear the pronunciation of the translated response.

6. Continue the Conversation

Keep chatting! Respond to the AI in English. The AI will remember the context of the conversation within the current session.

7. Reset

Click the Reset button () at any time to clear the chat and start over by selecting a new language or scenario.

Happy Learning!
